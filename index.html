Proteomics Analysis Workflows

This site provides the rendered HTML workflows and supporting code for the analyses reported in our manuscript "Single-subject Proteomic Signatures in Alzheimer’s Disease Reflect Clinical Phenotypes and Distinguish Asymptomatic from Symptomatic Cases". The reports cover TMT proteomics processing, single-subject differential expression analysis, pathway/subdomain profiling, graph-based clustering, and clinical correlation analyses.
